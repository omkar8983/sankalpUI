POST GRADUATE PROGRAM IN CLINICAL NUTRITION (PGPCN)
It's an 18-months online program on clinical nutrition which includes comprehensive coverage of topics around Human Physiology, Core concepts of Clinical Nutrition & in-depth focus on the Product Science of Signutra portfolio.

The course is designed exclusively for Signutra employees to increase self & organizational effectiveness by enhancing the scientific & conceptual knowledge; and increasing the overall understanding of the Signutra products. This will provide an excellent opportunity to the participants to refocus, and immerse themselves in the fundamentals of nutrition science and develop competencies in order to establish a confident, clear & credible communication with the HCPs.
